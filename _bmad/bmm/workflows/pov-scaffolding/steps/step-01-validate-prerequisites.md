# Step 01: Validate POV Prerequisites

**Goal:** Ensure the source PO/SOW and relevant strategic context are available to correctly map customer promises to the product roadmap.

---

## 1. PRE-ANALYSIS CHECKLIST
Verify the existence of the following:
- [ ] **PO or SOW:** The primary source document (PDF/Doc/Text).
- [ ] **Level 0 Initiative:** A corresponding Strategic Initiative in the `PMT` project.
- [ ] **PoC Context:** Search for existing `POC-[Customer]` or `PRESALES` project context (Hacks & Success Criteria).
- [ ] **Roadmap Access:** Visibility into `EF-INTERNAL` or other product projects.

---

## 2. SOURCE INTAKE
Ask the user to provide or point to the PO/SOW content. 

---

## 3. NEXT STEPS
Once the PO/SOW is received and basic context is established:
1. Confirm the Customer Project Key (e.g., `CUS-LLA`).
2. Halt and wait for user confirmation to proceed to **Step 02: Extract Deliverables**.
