# Step 03: Scaffold POV Jira Structure

**Goal:** Create the Level 1 Epic (PO) and Level 2 Stories (Commitments) in the Customer Jira Project.

---

## 1. SCAFFOLDING SEQUENCE
- **Level 1 Epic (PO):** Create a single Epic representing the entire PO.
    - **Epic Summary:** [Customer Name] - [PO Reference] Deployment.
    - **Work Category:** Mandatory.
- **Level 2 Stories (Commitments):** Create individual Stories under the PO Epic for each deliverable identified in Step 02.
    - **Story Summary:** [Deliverable Name].
    - **Labels:** `pov-draft`, `golden-chain-missing`.

---

## 2. JIRA AUTOMATION ACTION
Trigger or simulate the **Golden Chain Integrity Rule** to flag these new stories as missing a product link.
