# Step 03: Approval Processing

**Goal:** Coordinate the stakeholders' review and secure the final Go/No-Go decision.

---

## EXECUTION STEPS

### 1. Stakeholder Review
- Share the link/file path of the **PoC Intake RFC** generated in Step 02 with the designated approvers (Product Management, Engineering, and Sales).

### 2. Feedback Integration
- Collect and incorporate feedback on the **`poc-hack` strategy**.
- **Constraint:** If Engineering rejects a specific hack as "Too High Risk," the SA must propose an alternative implementation or a longer deadline.

### 3. Decision Capture
- **Approved:** Once all three sign-offs are received (indicated via Confluence Approval macro or user confirmation), **proceed to Step 04.**
- **Rejected:** Archive the RFC and document the reason for No-Go.

---

## OUTPUT CRITERIA
1. **Approval Confirmed:** Transition to the Jira creation phase.
2. **Revision Needed:** Update the RFC and repeat Step 03.
